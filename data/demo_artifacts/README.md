Small demo artifacts derived deterministically from the actual repo data.

Generated by scripts/make_demo_artifacts.py by taking the leftmost N sample columns (default 50) and keeping all genes.

Files:
- VIC_expr.small.tsv — subset of data/VIC_expr.tsv
- cells_expr.small.tsv — subset of data/cells_expr.tsv.tar.gz
- cells_samples_annot.small.tsv — matching subset of data/cells_samples_annot.tsv.tar.gz
- cancer_expr.small.tsv — subset of data/cancer_expr.tsv.tar.gz
- cancer_samples_annot.small.tsv — matching subset of data/cancer_samples_annot.tsv.tar.gz
- blood_expr.small.tsv — subset of data/validation_datasets/blood_expr.tsv

No random sampling is used; selection is deterministic to ensure reproducibility and provenance.
